Trainings

 
Sports training is a complex process that consists of organized exercise (work) aimed at improving the physical, mental, intellectual, technical and tactical qualities of exercisers (athletes).

In order for the training to be successful, all the principles of training must be respected (principle of individuality, overload, principle of specificity, continuity, diversity, principle of hard-easy, principle of periodization). At V&M, special attention is paid to the principle of individuality, which means that each exerciser is an individual and that training must be based on his individual characteristics, in addition to which specific problems happen to everyone over time (injuries, illnesses, emotional problems…). this fact must not be overlooked during training planning.
