Nutritionist

 
 
Nutritionist - The role of a nutritionist is to teach you how to eat properly without being hungry, exhausted and without energy. It is necessary to enter all food groups in order to satisfy all the needs of the organism. Proper and balanced diet includes intake of meat, milk and dairy products, fish, eggs, fruits, vegetables, cereals, sugars and oils. All this is necessary for our organism.
