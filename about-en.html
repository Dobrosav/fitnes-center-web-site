About

 
 
Gym and fitness club V&M is located in Zvezdara, surrounded by greenery, with a view of the park it certainly breaks the stereotype of suffocating, cramped exercise spaces. We create an atmosphere in which a pleasant environment gives a new experience where exercise is relaxed, safe and fun. We will follow you from the very beginning until you reach your fitness goals and our friendly staff is at your disposal for all advice on training and nutrition whether you are a recreational athlete or a serious athlete. In the area of ​​500m 2 you have cardio at your disposal, starter equipment, free weights as well as group programs of aerobics and pilates and comfortable locker rooms with showers. In our snack bar you will find drinks and chocolates for your better performance or post-workout recovery.
