Cardio

 
Cardio training is the most effective way to remove fat deposits, achieve and maintain fitness. These exercises are necessary for any effective training program, but they are also good for the overall health of the body! Also, if you haven't exercised for a long time, and you don't have the strength for other workouts, you can always adjust your cardio training to your abilities.
